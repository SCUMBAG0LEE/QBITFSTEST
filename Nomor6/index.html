About Mie Gacoan

Started in Solo, Central Java, Mie Gacoan has become one of Indonesia's most beloved noodle restaurants. We're famous for our perfectly spiced noodles and affordable prices that don't compromise on quality.

Our secret? Fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and a passion for serving happiness in every bowl!
